\# Social Rights Guidance System



This wiki explains how the Social Rights Guidance Platform works.



\## What is this system?



A digital guidance platform that helps individuals understand and access their social rights.



\## What does it provide?



\- eligibility guidance

\- clear explanations

\- next step directions



⚠️ This is not an official government decision system.



\---



\## System Structure



\- Frontend: user interface and guidance

\- Backend: decision engine (socialrightlabs)



> Backend decides, frontend renders.
